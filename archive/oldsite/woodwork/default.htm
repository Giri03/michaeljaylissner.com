Woodworking Portfolio

Padauk and Bubinga Grandfather Clock

            
This clock can safely be called my masterpiece. I spent several hours on it every day for two years, making everything from plywood for the gears to Sam Maloof style joinery in the Bubinga case.

Wall Clock

This was the first project that I made, and the one that really piqued my interest in woodworking. It's made of walnut, and took approximately 50 hours to complete with the oil finish.

Walnut Desk
   
This desk was a side project I took on while working on the grandfather clock, above. It took about four months and was also made from walnut with an oil finish. This project has a total of 36 mortise and tenon joints.

Purpleheart Shelves

Constructed exclusively from purpleheart with ebony plugs, this small shelf was quite challenging. Note the grain pattern on the bottom shelf.
