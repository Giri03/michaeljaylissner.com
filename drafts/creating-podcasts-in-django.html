Podcasts were created about a decade ago as a way for creators of audio and video content to share their work freely in a way that fans of a show could easily use to get updates whenever a new episode was created. Over the past decade, thousands of podcasts have been created (the current estimate is 115,000 English podcasts), but oddly, the tooling for podcast hosts such as myself remains fairly poor. After trawling the Internet and building podcast support into CourtListener, I’ve come up with a few tools, and I’ve written up some notes for an easy way to create podcasts in Django.

Making a Podcast with Django

There are currently two apps designed to make podcasting in Django easy, django-podcast and its successor of sorts, Django Podcasting. I dug into the code of both of these, but from my perspective, they both offer too much and this is a case where less is more. How hard, I ask you, is it to create an XML file linking to some MP3 files? Not that hard.
